Buying with BEVIKS Realty: How the Process Works

Step 1

Browse - Explore our available properties and identify the development that fits your goals and budget.

Step 2

Enquire - Reach out by phone, WhatsApp, or the contact form. A member of our team responds within one business day.

Step 3

Inspect - We'll arrange a site visit at a time that works for you. See the land, the infrastructure, the environment, in person.

Step 4

Reserve - Once you're ready, we secure your plot or unit with your initial deposit and issue your reservation documents.

Step 5

Complete - Payments are structured according to your chosen plan. Full documentation is issued upon completion.
