Entrepreneurship & Capacity Development Programs

SKDF runs entrepreneurship programs designed to equip participants with practical business knowledge and leadership capabilities.

EnableHer Initiative

EnableHer is one of SKDF’s flagship initiatives focused on empowering women entrepreneurs.

Through curated learning sessions, networking experiences, and mentorship conversations, EnableHer provides a supportive environment where women can explore business ideas, strengthen leadership capacity, and connect with other founders.

The initiative creates opportunities for participants to gain insights, share experiences, and access valuable entrepreneurial networks.

Skills Development & Creative Enterprise Training

SKDF recognizes that entrepreneurship can emerge through both knowledge-based and skill-based pathways.

Our skills development programs provide hands-on training in vocational and creative disciplines such as:

  • Fashion and apparel production

  • Creative craftsmanship

  • Production-based enterprises

Beyond technical training, participants also receive business education that helps them understand how to convert their skills into viable income-generating ventures.

This approach ensures that individuals transition from skill acquisition to sustainable earning opportunities.

Community-Based Learning & Engagement

SKDF places strong emphasis on community-driven learning.

We organize workshops, hangouts, and localized learning sessions that create safe and supportive environments where participants can:

  • Share experiences and challenges

  • Learn from peers

  • Build accountability structures

  • Collaborate on ideas and opportunities

These spaces help foster deeper engagement and long-term growth within the SKDF community.
